FAQ

Questions, answered straight

Is this the same as SEO?

No. SEO focuses on search results. This checks whether AI assistants name your business when someone asks for a recommendation. The disciplines overlap, but visibility in one does not guarantee visibility in the other.

Do you guarantee I'll show up in ChatGPT?

No — and anyone who guarantees that is lying. AI answers change across platforms and over time. What I provide is an honest, evidence-based snapshot of where you stand today and a prioritized plan for what to improve.

How fast do results come?

The audit itself is delivered in 3–5 days. Visibility can change over the following weeks after recommendations are implemented, but timing varies by platform and market.

I've never heard of AEO — is this real?

Yes. BrightLocal's 2026 survey found that 45% of consumers use generative AI for local-business recommendations, while SOCi found ChatGPT recommended 1.2% of brand locations in a large multi-location visibility study. AI visibility is already measurable — and highly selective.

What do you need from me?

Just your business name, primary market, website, and main services. A Google Business Profile link helps but isn't required.

Can you do the fixes for me?

Yes. After the audit you can take the fix list and run with it, or have me implement it — done-for-you fixes are a separate engagement, and your audit fee credits toward it.

The honest version

No guarantees. Just evidence.

AI answers change, and nobody can promise a permanent position. What I can provide is an evidence-based snapshot of where you stand, clear competitor context, and a prioritized plan. The diagnosis is real, and it is yours to keep.
